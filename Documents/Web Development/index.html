Sustainable Living Practices

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

Learn the three R's of waste management and how to implement them in your home.

  • Reduce your consumption of single-use plastics
  • Reuse containers and materials whenever possible
  • Recycle paper, glass, and approved plastics

Energy Conservation

Simple ways to reduce your energy consumption:

  1. Switch to LED light bulbs
  2. Unplug electronics when not in use
  3. Use energy-efficient appliances
